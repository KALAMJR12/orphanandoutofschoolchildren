Our Mission

Education Initiative for Every Child

The H.E Atiku Abubakar Foundation In Collaboration With Sheik Ahmad Tijjani Umar Foundation is proud to launch this comprehensive education initiative in honor of Alhaji Atiku Abubakar's 80th birthday. Our mission is to ensure that every orphan and out-of-school child has access to quality education from primary through secondary level.
